The Annex

*Annex price: $200 flat fee for one server or bartender with no sales minimum.

  • Good for groups of any size up to 30 cocktail style or 20 seated.
  • Server/Bartender package available.
  • Private single bathroom.
  • Full sound system with hook ups for most equipment.
  • Wifi.
  • Large screen TV with HDMI, VGA and RCA hookups.
  • Private street access.
  • Great for catered mingle events, movies and tv show viewings, intimate dinner parties, baby showers and sporting events.
